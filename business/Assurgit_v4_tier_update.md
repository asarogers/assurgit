# Assurgit Tier Update (March 2026)

This file supersedes **Section 6 (Pricing and Tier Logic)** of Assurgit_v4.docx.
All other sections of v4 remain in effect.

## Decision

Scale is hidden until the client volume supports it. Features have been shifted up one tier to reduce complexity, lower the operational burden of lower tiers, and make the offer ladder easier to close.

## New Tier Structure

| Plan | Price | Best for | Key feature unlocks |
|------|-------|----------|---------------------|
| **Launch** | $397/mo | Local service businesses, price-sensitive entry buyers | 5 videos/wk, 4 platforms, MP4 delivery, research-backed scripts, avatar + voice clone, human QC, 48hr review window |
| **Starter** ⭐ | $997/mo | Coaches, consultants, loan officers, advisors | Everything in Launch + **auto-publishing**, **weekly competitive intel briefing**, **Friday strategy call** |
| **Growth** | $1,997/mo | Teams, agencies, multi-location or white-glove accounts | Everything in Starter + **10+ videos/wk**, **white-glove service**, **priority support**, **monthly business review call** |
| ~~Scale~~ | ~~$3,497/mo~~ | *Hidden — reintroduce when client volume supports it* | — |

## Tier logic (updated)

**Platform reach** — All tiers include 4 platforms (IG, TT, LI, YT). Delivery method is the differentiator: Launch delivers MP4 via email; Starter and Growth auto-publish.

**Automation** — Auto-publishing unlocks at **Starter** (previously Growth). This is the primary automation unlock.

**Support and strategy** — Friday strategy call and weekly competitive intel brief unlock at **Starter** (previously Growth). White-glove + monthly review + priority support remain at **Growth**.

**Volume** — 5 videos/wk at Launch and Starter. 10+ videos/wk at Growth.
